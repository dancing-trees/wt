-----History----

Dennis Ritchie was born on September 9, 1941.He created the C programming language with his mate Brian Kernighan in the early 70s. He was one of the developers of the Unix operating system. His contribution in the Book The C programming language is still remebered by college students and scholars. Ritchie was found dead on October 12, 2011 at his home in New Jersey where he lived alone.

His work was a miracle, for future and its technology.
