-----History----

Bjarne stroustrup born on 30 December 1950 is a Danish Computer scientist. He is known for his creation, C++. He is serving as a professor in Columbia University. His creation C++ is a upgraded version of C. Bjarne added object oriented programmming, Function, operator and the list goes on. Famous Browsers like firefox, google chrome, safari are created with the help of C++. Game engines are written down in C++ due to its capability to work with hardware-level elements.


We are grateful for C++, which introduces humanity to a new world of reality
